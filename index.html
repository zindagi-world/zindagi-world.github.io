About Us

Zindagi is a youth activist organization created with the aim of community building, spreading awareness, and educating people regarding socio-economic issues which concern certain sections of the society. Our organisation believes community outreach is an effective medium by which we can implement these plans. We plan to raise capital through fundraisers which will aid our efforts to educate and reach out to the target audience. The projects which our organisation will undertake represent small steps in the path to creating an easier world to live in.

Menstrual Drive

Zindagi as an organization, along with the Rotaract Club of Caduceus and Enactus HR, has started a project to create menstrual hygiene awareness in economically backward areas. This is to improve access to information in the related field and make the products accessible to the target audience. We aim to target low income communities, educate children under resourced schools and homeless communities to create a better, well defined awareness on menstrual hygiene and its importance. Zindagi will be purchasing the menstrual kits for Rs. 250 and distributing them. The kits are prepared by women who are familiar with the requirements of those living in rural areas.

By promoting the use of cloth sanitary napkins we also aim to create an eco-friendly poise to battle environment change and global warming.Single use plastic is disposed of in landfills. When the waste in landfills decomposes, it releases methane, which is orders of magnitude worse than carbon dioxide in trapping heat in the atmosphere. By minimising the use of the same and shifting our focus to cloth based sanitary products, our organisation seeks to promote sustainable products and ensure that there arenâ€™t any adverse effects on the environment. We are working on creating modules and fundraising guides to enable us to conduct these educational programs to the best of our abilities. It must be noted that a high percentage of the population in India has distorted views regarding menstruation. Zindagi aims to raise awareness and educate people about the same in order to correct mistaken notions they may have.
